Short Bio

I am currently pursuing a Master's degree in Computer Technology at Wuhan University, where I am advised by Prof. Bo Du. I received my B.S. degree in Computer Science and Technology (Hongyi Program) from Wuhan University in 2025.

My research focuses on Medical AI, Federated Learning, Multimodal Learning, and Vision-Language Models. More broadly, I am interested in building robust and practical intelligent systems for privacy-constrained healthcare settings.
